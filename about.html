What is SimpleStopwatch?

What is it for?
SimpleStopwatch is meant to be a productivity app that tracks how much time you are spending on certain tasks, and then reports the data back to you. Simply choose a tag to categorize how you are spending your next session, and then start the watch! When you are done, pause and click save, and the time will be automatically stored in the website's data, along with the time of day. In the evening, you can then check your statistics to see how much time you've spent doing certain things.

In addition, you can set goals for your target amount of time you want to spend on a certain subject per day, and SimpleStopwatch will automatically give you a grades based on how you're doing, both for that category specifically and for how well you are meeting your goals in general.
Anything Else?
This is a very work in progress website, so a lot of things can definitely be added. We're thinking of things like adding tasks to check off, possibly a built in timer/pomodoro timer, and maybe other things like schedules to plan out what you'll be doing. However, we obviously have to finish the bare bones of it all before going on to these aspects of the website anyway.
